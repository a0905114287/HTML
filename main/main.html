Glock19

I've created a 3D model of the Glock 19, a compact and reliable 9mm pistol known for its durability and simplicity. The model features a polymer frame, steel slide, and a 15-round magazine capacity. It accurately represents the iconic Safe Action® system with a striker-fired mechanism, offering realistic details in both form and function. This model can be used for visualizations, simulations, or as a design reference.
